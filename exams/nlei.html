Necessary Knowledge

To pass the NLE I exam, you need to know:

  • For this section, you need to be able to understand mythology, history, and culture! You need to be able to understand certain figures and their ambitions, while also having the knowledge for the mythological figures such as Zeus and Hera. In addition, you need to be able to locate certain locations on the map such as Rome and Sicily. Finally, you need to be able to uderstand declensions, conjugations, and sentence structures to a higher level. Make sure to practice the exams!
