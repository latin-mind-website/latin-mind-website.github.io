Necessary Knowledge

To pass the NLE intro:

  • Understand the symbol pertaining to the Greek gods/goddesses
  • Understand Greek and Roman names of gods/goddesses, with the addition of their history
  • Be able to locate certain Empires/Capitals in a map
  • Be able to understand basic declensions/conjugations
  • Understand Basic Vocab list
  • Understand basic culture such as colloseum, pantheon, etc.
