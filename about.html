About Me

Let me introduce myself

Hi, I'm Rayhan

Rayhan Roswendi is a curious mind and a passionate learner with a love for both the ancient and the modern. Rayhan is captivated by Latin, drawn not only to its language but to the mythological stories it holds—tales that have fascinated him since he was young. His interest in mythology fuels his enthusiasm for Latin, making it a rich and meaningful pursuit.

Beyond the classics, Rayhan has spent the past two years as a dedicated member of the golf team. While he plays golf recreationally, he thrives on the camaraderie of the team and the challenge of perfecting his swing.

Rayhan’s intellectual curiosity extends to coding, where he’s been immersing himself in Python and Java. He is intrigued by their inherent capabilities and the intricate connections they allow him to create. This appreciation for complexity mirrors his artistic side: for over five years, Rayhan has played the piano, using music as a powerful way to express his emotions. While he might not have composed the music he plays, the ability to bring beautiful melodies to life brings him immense joy and a sense of accomplishment.

In his free time, Rayhan enjoys the energy and competition of basketball with his friends, as well as unwinding with video games like Minecraft. And when the day is done, he delights in the simple pleasure of sleeping—resting his active mind and recharging for whatever adventure comes next.
